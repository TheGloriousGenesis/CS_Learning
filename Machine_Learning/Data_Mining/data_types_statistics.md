# When to use certain types of statistics

Certain statistics types depend on the [type of data](./data_mining.md#data-types)

## Categorical

### Ordinal
- frequencies
- proportions
- percentages
- percentiles
- median
- mode
- interquartile range


## Numerical

### Discrete
- rate of occurence
- summary of count
- mean
- sum
- standard deviation

### Continuous
- most of the above!
- percentiles
- median
- interquartile range
- mean
- mode
- standard deviation
- range
